Infos about OutandOpen

OutandOpen is an app that aimes to help with your outdoor tours. At the early state the app is still in its functionality is mostly helping to plan mountain hiking tours on food. And even in that area the help the app can give you is rather limited. However, lets see how this app evolves. Flow @ountandopen on twitter for updates.

A few words on navigation: whenever you see the i on a button or in a header you get backround information. The "home" sign in the headers brings you to the front page.

At the moment no monitary support is needed but you can show your appreciation by flattering OutandOpen

Profile

To fillout your profile will help to find mountain tours for you, which you might enjoy. You can share your profile with your friends or make it public but you don't need to.

In the future there may be sophisticated ways to figure out how fit and experienced you are. Up to now you have to rate yourselfe.

Physical Fitness

Rate your physical fitness on a scale from 0 ("I am almost dead") to 10 ("I could start an himalaya expedition right now.").

As a roule of thumb you could devide the maximum altitude differenc you walked on a singel day by 300 (e.g tour starting at 500m reaching the peak at 1700m gives you a rating of 4).

climbing experience

Rate how experienced your are with mountains on a scale from 0 ("I have never seen one") to 10 ("I did the seven summits and some big walls."). If you have no clue what the "seven summits" are, you are probably closer to 0 then to 10 ;-).

Fitness

Rate how compftable you are when it comes to hights. 0 means you freak out when you think of a mountain; 10 if you could walk on a slagline 500m abouve the ground without the slightest arousal.").
